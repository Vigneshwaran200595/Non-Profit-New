Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

How does my donation help children in need?

+

Your donation provides food, clean water, education, medical care and safe shelter for vulnerable children in underdeveloped communities.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

+

Yes, all donations made to our registered charity organization are fully tax-deductible as per government regulations.

Can I volunteer with your organization?

+

Yes! We welcome volunteers for teaching programs, medical camps, fundraising events, and community outreach activities.

How do you choose which communities to support?

+

We prioritize areas with limited access to education, healthcare, food security, and safe housing, based on field research and local partnerships.

Can I sponsor a child monthly?

+

Yes, through our Child Sponsorship Program you can support a child’s education, nutrition, and daily needs every month.

Where does the charity spend most of its funds?

+

Over 85% of funds directly support charity projects like food relief, education, medical care, and emergency aid services.
